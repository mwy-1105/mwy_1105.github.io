About

I am the IEEE Student Member and CCF Student Member.

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in software engineering at the Software Engineering Institute (Dishui Lake International Software Engineering Institute) and Shanghai Key Laboratory of Trustworthy Computing, East China Normal University (ECNU), advised by Prof. Jing Liu.

Prior to this, I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from the School of Computer and Information Engineering and Institute for Artificial Intelligence, Shanghai Polytechnic University (SSPU), advised by Prof. Wenan Tan.

My research focuses on formal methods in software engineering, model checking, social computing and adaptive collaborating system, intelligent industrial IoT.
